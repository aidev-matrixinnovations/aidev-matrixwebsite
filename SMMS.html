The Social Media Landscape

Key Platforms

  • Facebook: 2.8 billion MAU
  • Instagram: 1.3 billion MAU
  • Twitter: 330 million MAU
  • LinkedIn: 740 million users
  • TikTok: 1 billion MAU
  • Pinterest: 440 million MAU

Why Social Media Marketing?

  • 54% of social browsers use social media to research products (GlobalWebIndex)
  • 71% of consumers who have a positive social media experience with a brand are likely to recommend it (Lyfemarketing)
  • Social media has a 100% higher lead-to-close rate than outbound marketing (HubSpot)

Paid Social Advertising

Ad Types by Platform

  • Facebook: Image, Video, Carousel, Collection
  • Instagram: Stories, Reels, IGTV
  • Twitter: Promoted Tweets, Trends
  • LinkedIn: Sponsored Content, InMail

Targeting Options

  • Demographics: Age, gender, location, education
  • Interests: Hobbies, favorite entertainment, shopping habits
  • Behaviors: Purchase history, device usage
  • Connections: Friends of followers, lookalike audiences
  • Retargeting: Website visitors, app users, engagement
